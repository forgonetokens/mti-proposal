What This Proposal Is About

This proposal requests board approval for a pilot program to build simple, focused digital tools that make it easier for members to access their benefits and for officers to serve them.

The Pilot Will:

  • Deploy tools that are already built and ready to use
  • Develop additional tools based on member and officer needs
  • Report back to the board with results and a recommendation

What This Is Not:

  • Not a request for a large budget â€” pilot is capped at $500

What's at Stake

For Members

Experience shapes perception.

When benefits feel like a hassle, it erodes confidence in membership. A member who gives up on a claim learns the union makes life harder, not easier.

  • Members leave money on the table
  • Frustration with paperwork becomes frustration with the union

For Officers & Staff

Busywork crowds out strategy.

Every hour chasing documents is an hour not spent on organizing, contracts, or building power. We become reactive instead of proactive.

  • Officers burn hours on tasks that could be streamlined
  • Less time for outreach and planning

This pilot is about reclaiming time â€” for members to get what they've earned, and for officers to build our future.

The Opportunity

What We're Building

  • Simple web apps that solve specific problems, not complex software systems.
  • Focused tools that do one thing well and are accessible from any device.
  • Tools that empower officers and staff rather than replacing them.

This Isn't About Reinventing How We Operate

This is surgical process improvement at specific friction points where members and staff lose time.

Small, targeted fixes create outsized impact on member experience and officer workload.

"Accessing what members have earned should feel simple and dignified."

Proof of Concept Already Built

The MRP Claimer helps members draft, combine receipts, and submit claims to receive copay reimbursement.

Before

Print the form, fill by hand, scan it back. Tedious enough that some members didn't bother.

After

One web form, attach receipts, submit. Save your profile to skip repetitive entry next time.
